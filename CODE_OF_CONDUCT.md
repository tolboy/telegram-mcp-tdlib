# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity or expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected behavior

Be respectful, give constructive feedback, assume good intent, protect other people's privacy, and keep Telegram credentials or private content out of public discussions.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discriminatory language, intimidation, publishing private information without permission, and other conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting are not tolerated.

## Enforcement

Report concerns through the repository's private security reporting channel. Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct.

This policy is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/), version 2.1.
